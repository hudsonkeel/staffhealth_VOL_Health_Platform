  • Why VI: Make endemic volatility visible in one stability score and economic range.
  • How it works: Ten inputs roll into a 0–100 stability score and directional annual economics.

Workforce Stability Index (WSI)
Companion view focused on workforce pipeline stability and drag.
›
Scores staffing volatility, financial exposure, operational & compliance risk, and workforce pipeline health into a single WSI score.
  • Why WSI: Quantify how unstable schedules, vacancies, and turnover drag operations and margin.
  • How it works: Mix of direct inputs or portfolio distributions roll up into a 0–100 WSI.
  • Details: Shows volatility cost, stabilizable upside, and workforce drag as dollar ranges.
